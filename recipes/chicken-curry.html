Chicken Curry

Description

This Chicken Curry recipe features tender chicken pieces simmered in a rich, flavorful curry sauce with a blend of spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger, and cook until onions are translucent.
  2. Add curry powder and cook for another minute.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Pour in coconut milk, chicken broth, and diced tomatoes. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
